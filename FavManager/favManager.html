My Favourite Manager of all time is Zinedine Zidane

Zinedine Zidane was born on 23 June 1972, popularly known as Zizou, is a French professional football manager and former player who played as an attacking midfielder. He most recently coached Spanish club Real Madrid and is one of the most successful coaches in the world.
