Cristiano Ronaldo

My Favourite player of all time is Critiano Ronaldo

He was born on 5th February 1985 and is a Portuguese-born professional football player who captains the Portugal squad and plays forward for Al Nassr in the Saudi Professional League. Ronaldo, who is recognized as one of the all-time best players, owns the record for the most European player awards with four European Golden Shoes and five Ballon d'Or trophies. Together with seven league championships, five UEFA Champions Leagues, the UEFA European Championship, and the UEFA Nations League, he has won 32 trophies overall during the course of his career.
